## Welcome to the Blog

This is the beginning of something *raw* and *bold*. No fancy gradients, no subtle shadows — just pure, unfiltered content.

### Why Neobrutalism?

Neobrutalism is a design movement that embraces:

- *Harsh contrasts* over subtle gradients
- *Bold borders* instead of soft shadows
- *Raw aesthetics* that feel honest
- *Intentional roughness* as a feature, not a bug

### What to Expect

I'll be writing about:

1. Web development and modern frameworks
2. Design philosophy and UI/UX
3. Random thoughts and observations
4. Code snippets and tutorials

Stay tuned for more content. The brutalist revolution starts now.
